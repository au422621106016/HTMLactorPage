vijay

Vijay, also known as Thalapathy, is a celebrated Indian actor and politician, renowned for his work in Tamil cinema and his recent entry into politics. Early Life and Background Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar was born on June 22, 1974, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, to film director S. A. Chandrasekhar and playback singer Shoba Chandrasekhar. He had a younger sister, Vidhya, who passed away at the age of two, and his production house, V.V Productions, is named in her memory. Vijay began his education at Fathima School in Kodambakkam and later attended Loyola College, earning a Bachelor’s degree in Visual Communication.
